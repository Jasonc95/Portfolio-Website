I'm a firm believer in the saying "healthy body, healthy mind". I love to swimming in the ocean with my snorkel, diving down amongst schools of fish and soaking in peacefulness of the underwater world.

Of course, gaming. Although I'm more than fond of the outdoors you will catch me gaming from time to time, because I travel its a great way for me to stay connected and somewhat social with my friends. My favourite game I play is Rocket League seconded by Call of Duty.
